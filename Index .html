Volleyball is a sport that people all over the world love!
It has some simple rules but playing it well takes skill and teamwork.
Volleyball needs a lot of practise.



EQUIPMENT

1:The BALL: A hard leather or synthetic ball, usually white or bright-colored.
2:The NET: A high-tension net that stretches across the middle of the court, separating the two teams.
3:The Court: A rectangular playing surface, divided into front and back rows for each team.
4: Poles & Antennae: Two poles hold the net up, with flexible rods (antennae) on the sides marking the legal playing space.

What PLAYERS USE:

1:¬∑ Athletic Shoes: Lightweight court shoes with good grip for quick lateral moves and jumps.
2:¬∑ Knee Pads: Worn to protect knees during dives and digs on the floor.
3:Comfortable Clothing: Usually a jersey and shorts or spandex that allow for full range of motion.
4:Ankle Braces (Optional): Some players wear them for extra support and to help prevent injuries.



PLAYERS:

There are total of six(6) players in each team.
Three main players are called SPIKER, SERVER AND SETTER.

Other three players are LIBERO, BLOCKER and DEFENSIVE SPECIALIST.

Player's positions:

Players are positioned in two rows on the court:
Three players stand in the front row near the net, and three players stand in the back row.
Front-row players attack and block, while back-row players focus on defense and receiving the ball.




BASIC RULES:

1:A team can only touch the ball upto THREE(3) times before sending it to the other team.
2:The same player cannot hit the ball twice in a row (except after a block).
3:The same player cannot hit the ball twice in a row (except after a block).
4:A point is scored on every rally, no matter who served.
5:The ball is out if it lands outside the court lines.
6:Touching the net is strictly prohibited.
7:A serve must be hit from behind the back line.
8:Matches are played in sets, and the team that wins more sets WINS the match!
